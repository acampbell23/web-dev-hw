Best Taco Recipe

Ingredients

Esimated time about 1hr 30 to prepare

  • 1 lb ground beef 70-80% lean
  • 1 tbsp chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce

Directions

  1. Step 1: Brown the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Step 2: Add chili powder, salt, cumin, dried oregano, garlic powder, and onion powder to the skillet. Stir well
  4. Step 3: Pour in the tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Step 4: Warm up the corn totillas in a seprate pan.
  6. Step 5: Serve the seasoned ground beef in the warmed tortillas and enjoy!

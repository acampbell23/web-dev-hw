Open-Face Breakfast Sandwitch Recipe

This is my personal favorite way to eat my eggs in the morning. This recipe is Quick, Easy, Healthy?, and Delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of bread
  • 1 onion and green pepper carmolized (prepare and reserve for multiple meals)
  • 1-2 eggs
  • 1/2tsp butter
  • 1-2 slices of sharp white cheddar cheese
  • couple slices of tomato
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • (your choice of hotsauce)

Directions

Video

  1. Step 1: Place bread in toaster (start toasting when you drop eggs to cook)
  2. Step 2: Warm pan to medium heat and add caramelized veggies
  3. Step 3: Once veggies are hot push them to the side of the pad to leave room to cook eggs
  4. Step 4: Crack 1-2 eggs into the pan and cook 1-3 minutes each side
  5. Step 5: Turn off heat and add cheese to pan to melt
  6. Step 6: Take toast and add butter
  7. Step 7: Place tomato slices on toast and season with salt/pepper
  8. Step 8: Add your hot caramelized veggies
  9. Step 9: Add eggs and melted cheese on top
  10. Step 10: Enjoy!
